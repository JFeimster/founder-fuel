### Recommended brand name
**Founder Fuel**

### Backup options
- **Runway Manual**
- **Capital Reality Check**

### Brand thesis
Most early-stage founders are not failing because they lack hustle. They are failing to get funded because they are being sold funding narratives that do not match their current stage. Founder Fuel exists to replace startup funding fantasy with stage-based reality, readable strategy, and conversion paths built on trust.

### One-liner
Real startup funding guidance for founders who are too early for bank mythology.

### Short positioning statement
Founder Fuel is a neo-brutalist editorial fintech brand that helps early-stage founders understand realistic funding options, qualification pathways, and capital tradeoffs in the first 24 months.

### What this site is / what it is not
**This site is:** an educational, conversion-aware startup funding media property for realistic funding paths.  
**This site is not:** a hype lender, fake approval funnel, or generic loan broker.

## Phase 1 — Visual direction decision

### Primary design system choice
**Reference Style B is the primary visual direction.** The uploaded neo-brutalist sample is the right foundation for Founder Fuel because it already expresses anti-corporate, founder-native, high-contrast fintech energy.

### Why it fits
- It matches the anti-bank, anti-gatekeeper tone
- It makes stage-based cards and comparison modules feel punchy
- It avoids the polite SaaS blandness that would weaken the positioning
- It supports a media/editorial voice with stronger visual opinion

### Traits to carry forward
- Heavy borders
- Offset shadows
- Bold uppercase display typography
- Loud card treatments
- High-contrast CTA treatment
- Punchy modular blocks for “reality checks”

### Traits to avoid
- Visual chaos that damages readability
- Too many competing accent colors in one viewport
- Overly compressed type
- Brutalism that feels amateur instead of intentional

### Small accents allowed from Reference A
- Cleaner editorial spacing
- Better longform readability
- Tighter comparison tables
- More controlled page rhythm for SEO articles

### Final direction
**Neo-Brutalist Editorial Fintech**  
Founder insurgency fintech, but organized like a serious editorial system.

Used to counter founder assumptions with direct plain-English statements.

High-contrast bordered tables for tradeoffs, stage fit, and qualification notes.

`details/summary` for static accessibility and minimal JS.

### CTA sections
Acid yellow or black-backed strong conversion band with paired primary/secondary actions.

Uppercase category label, bold title, brief deck, tag pills.

- Kicker
- Display headline

- CTA pair

### Mobile UX priorities
- Keep brutalism simple and readable
- Preserve clear tap targets
- Use stacked cards instead of dense layouts
